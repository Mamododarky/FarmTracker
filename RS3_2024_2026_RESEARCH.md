# RS3 farming research used for v8

Only RuneScape 3 sources and material dated 2024–2026 were used for new decisions in this build.

## Manor Farm

- RuneScape Wiki Player-owned farm quick guide, last updated 23 November 2024: current stage timings and the distinction between adolescent for beans and elder for XP.
- September 2025 r/runescape discussion: keep breeding pairs, keep troughs filled, and use elder for XP/resources.
- The app therefore defaults to chinchompas, zygomites, and dragons, uses Elder as the XP goal, and adds alarms for every stage.

Exact earliest-stage totals used:
- Chinchompas: 42 hours
- Zygomites: 84 hours
- Dragons: 168 hours

## Recent official changes

- 6 May 2025 Farming Strike: 4,000 Farming reputation now increases the chance of obtaining unchecked animals; Farming 114 alone unlocks the fourth spirit tree.
- 23 March 2026 Havenhythe launch and later 2026 farming-patch references: the Havenhythe nursery stops remain in the route.
- 13 April 2026 Aura Overhaul: Greenfingers is no longer an aura to activate; its relevant produce effect became a permanent Farming unlock. It is not shown in the checklist.

## Recent route discussion

- June 2026 r/runescape farm-run discussion continued to recommend highest-level animals and trees for low-attention XP, with carambola/growth-potion methods for faster bean-funded XP. The compact app keeps the XP-focused trees and animals rather than adding low-XP allotment clutter.
